Welcome!

Birds are those living beings that can fly high in the sky. Birds have big feathers that help them to lift their bodies in the air. There are hundreds of types of birds on Earth and each of them is unique. For example, the peacock has a magnificent tail made of beautiful feathers, the parrot is a bird that can learn to talk, crow is the smartest in bird kingdom, a hummingbird can sing, nightingale bird is also known for its melodious voice. Like this each bird is different in its abilities and looks. They are a very important part of our ecosystem and the food chain.
